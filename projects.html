Regional History Museum - Plovdiv | Digital Transformation & Web Accessibility

A complete digital overhaul for one of Bulgaria’s most significant cultural institutions. The mission was to transform the Regional History Museum - Plovdiv’s online presence into a high-end, visually immersive experience that matches the museum's prestige, while ensuring 100% legal compliance with international accessibility standards.

High-End Visual Identity: * Designed and developed a custom WordPress Child Theme with extensive CSS overrides to achieve a sophisticated, "museum-grade" aesthetic. Performed professional video and image post-production to ensure all multimedia content meets high-fidelity standards, reflecting the museum’s historical value.

Web Accessibility & Legal Compliance (WCAG): * The Challenge: The museum’s previous website failed to meet the mandatory Accessibility Declaration standards (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines - WCAG), resulting in legal penalties and fines. The Solution: Engineered the site from the ground up to be fully accessible to users with visual impairments and other disabilities. The Result: Following a rigorous re-inspection by the authorities, the new site passed with perfect scores, completely resolving all prior legal and compliance issues.
Performance & UI/UX: Created an intuitive navigation system that allows visitors to explore Plovdiv’s rich history effortlessly, with a focus on fast loading times and mobile responsiveness.
